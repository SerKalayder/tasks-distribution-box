Tasks distribution box

This is the simple page that may help you to organize your tasks. The box on the right divided into four subboxes due to different types of tasks:

A important and immediate

B important but not immediate

C not important, but immediate

D not important and not immediate

Start by adding all the task to the A box. Then anylize those tasks and move particular task one by one to the most appropriate subbox. Hence you get to know what tasks to do first.

When you are done, don't forget to preserve your tasks distribution to your favorite notebook.
